Purpose and Highlights

For the last two and a half years, my goal has been to make student and teacher experience of mathematics exponentially greater through the use of digital interactives. I challenge myself and my colleagues to make curriculum that is accessible to students of all cultures, backgrounds, and abilities through my work on the Access and Equity committee and my innovations in the creation of accessible digital material. I consistently support and nurture growth in my colleagues by providing consistent feedback and offering help wherever possible.
