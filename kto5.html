Growth

  • Started K-5 working under Scott and Richard
  • Initially worked to create applets that Scott ideated in grades 3 and 4
  • Applets were often based on ideas from writers
  • In G4 and 5, differentiated myself and took control of K-5 during the G5 writing process
  • Created progressively more complex applets, moving from strict digital model replication to more creative applets of my own design
  • Due to the popularity of the applets from G4 and G5, retroactively created applets for K-2, some of which made it into the TE, others missed the cut offs because production timelines had already been created
  • Documented and maintained databases of K-5 demos
  • Became go-to person for questions related to K-5 demos, their capabilities, and their uses
